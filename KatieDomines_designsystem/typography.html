Typography

The typography consists of IBM Plex Sans and IBM Mono Sans. IBM Plex was designed by Mike Abbink from IBM BX&D, in collaboration with Bold Monday, a Dutch type foundry. It is a Grotesque style typeface that includes a Sans, Sans Condensed, and Mono. This website uses both the Sans and Mono type faces.
