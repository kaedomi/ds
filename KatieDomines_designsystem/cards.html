Cards

There are two different types of cards. The first card contains a box with text and link buttons only. The second type of type card cotntains a box with an image, text, and occasionally link buttons. Read below to see which instances they would be used in.

Accessibility

Ensure that text has a high contrast against the card background color. Ensure that any images inside cards have an alt-text. Avoid too much functionality and reduce tab stops. To read more about cards and accessibility, click here.

This is the use of a card with text only. This would be used in sections of the about me page or sections of a case study of this portfolio. A link/button may also be omitted.
